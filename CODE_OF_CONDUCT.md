# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Resurgence is dedicated to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for everyone in our community. We are committed to fostering a culture of respect, safety, and collaboration regardless of:

- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socioeconomic status
- Nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation
- Marital and family status
- Political affiliation

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, both online and offline, including but not limited to:
- GitHub repositories and discussions
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Issues and comments
- Community events and meetings
- Social media channels

## Our Values

### Respect
We respect the dignity, opinions, and contributions of every community member. Disagreement should be expressed constructively and respectfully.

### Inclusion
We actively welcome and encourage participation from people of all backgrounds. We strive to make Resurgence accessible to everyone.

### Integrity
We act with honesty and transparency in all our interactions. We take responsibility for our actions.

### Collaboration
We work together toward our shared mission: using technology to improve disaster relief and save lives.

### Safety
We prioritize psychological and physical safety for all community members.

## Expected Behavior

We expect all community members to:

1. **Be Respectful**
   - Use inclusive language
   - Listen actively to others
   - Acknowledge different perspectives
   - Respect boundaries

2. **Be Professional**
   - Focus on ideas, not people
   - Provide constructive feedback
   - Assume good intent
   - Address disagreements privately when appropriate

3. **Be Collaborative**
   - Help each other succeed
   - Share knowledge generously
   - Support newcomers
   - Celebrate each other's contributions

4. **Be Responsible**
   - Report issues promptly
   - Take accountability for mistakes
   - Learn from feedback
   - Respect intellectual property and privacy

5. **Be Inclusive**
   - Welcome people from all backgrounds
   - Create space for diverse voices
   - Amplify underrepresented perspectives
   - Challenge exclusionary language or behavior

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

### Harassment
- Offensive comments, jokes, or slurs related to protected characteristics
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Deliberate intimidation or threats
- Stalking or repeated unwanted contact
- Publishing private information without consent
- Doxxing or revealing identifying information

### Discrimination
- Exclusion based on identity or background
- Denying opportunities based on prejudice
- Unequal treatment
- Microaggressions or subtle discrimination

### Abuse
- Personal attacks or insults
- Intentional disruption of discussions
- Spreading misinformation or disinformation
- Deliberate cruelty
- Abusive language

### Misconduct
- Sexual harassment or assault
- Unwanted touching
- Retaliation against those who report misconduct
- Obstruction of complaint processes
- Repeated violations after warning

### Violations of Privacy
- Non-consensual sharing of private information
- Recording conversations without permission
- Sharing content created in private settings
- Blackmail or coercion

## Reporting and Enforcement

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **If safe to do so, address it directly**
   - Tell the person their behavior is inappropriate
   - Be clear about what behavior is problematic
   - Give them an opportunity to respond

2. **If direct confrontation isn't safe or comfortable, report it:**
   - Email: [conduct@resurgence.org](mailto:conduct@resurgence.org)
   - GitHub Issue: Create a private security advisory
   - Private message: Contact a maintainer directly

3. **In your report, include:**
   - Description of what happened
   - Who was involved (if comfortable sharing)
   - When and where it occurred
   - Any witnesses
   - Any relevant links or screenshots (if safe)
   - How this behavior affected you

### Investigation Process

1. **Receipt**: We acknowledge your report within 48 hours
2. **Investigation**: We investigate promptly and fairly
3. **Communication**: We keep you informed (while respecting privacy)
4. **Resolution**: We take appropriate action
5. **Follow-up**: We check in after the resolution

### Confidentiality

We respect your privacy:
- Reports are confidential
- Only necessary people are involved in investigations
- We won't disclose your identity without permission
- We protect reporters from retaliation

### Possible Actions

Depending on severity and response:
- Private conversation
- Public apology
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent removal from community
- Reporting to authorities (if legal violations occur)

## Scope and Jurisdiction

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All Resurgence community spaces and events
- Interactions about Resurgence
- Contributions to Resurgence projects

We may take action on behavior outside our spaces if:
- It affects safety in our community
- It violates our values
- It demonstrates a pattern of harmful behavior

## Appeal Process

If you believe you were treated unfairly:
1. Email [conduct@resurgence.org](mailto:conduct@resurgence.org) with:
   - Description of the action taken
   - Why you believe it was unfair
   - What outcome you're seeking
2. An independent reviewer will evaluate your appeal
3. You'll receive a response within 5 business days

## Community Leaders

Community leaders (maintainers, moderators) are responsible for:
- Modeling this Code of Conduct
- Enforcing standards fairly and consistently
- Being available to help address issues
- Recusing themselves from situations with conflicts of interest
- Protecting confidentiality

## Specific Scenarios

### Code Review Feedback

**Acceptable:**
- "This approach has a potential security issue. Consider..."
- "I tested this and found it doesn't handle X case."
- "Great start! Here's how we might improve..."

**Unacceptable:**
- "This is terrible code. You clearly don't know what you're doing."
- "Anyone with brain cells would do this differently."
- Sarcasm or mockery about someone's technical ability

### Disagreements

**Acceptable:**
- "I respectfully disagree because..."
- "Here's a different perspective to consider..."
- "I see your point, but I still think..."

**Unacceptable:**
- "You're clearly wrong and stupid."
- Personal attacks mixed with technical disagreement
- Dismissive language about someone's opinion

### Feedback on Features

**Acceptable:**
- "This feature doesn't align with the project goals because..."
- "I'm concerned about the user experience impact of..."
- "Have you considered an alternative approach..."

**Unacceptable:**
- "This is a waste of time."
- "Only an idiot would use this feature."
- Questioning someone's competence instead of the idea
